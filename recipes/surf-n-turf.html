Surf n' Turf

Ingredients Needed

  • 1lb raw shrimp
  • 2 lbs steak of your choice at 1" thick
  • 2 Tbsp salted butter
  • 4 Tbsp olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder

Steps

The steak

  1. Set steaks out for at least 30 minutes, allowing the steak to reach room temperature
  2. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees Farenheight
  3. Set your stove top to medium-high heat, and place a cast iron pan on choice on the burner
  4. Allow the cast iron pan to heat to desired temperature (about 10 minutes)
  5. Once your steaks have reached room temperature, use a brush to generously brush steaks with olive oil
  6. Season with desired spices on both sides, and gently place your steaks in the pan for 3 minutes per side
  7. Place the pan with your steaks into the oven for about 5-6 minutes, depending on thickness
  8. set steaks on the stove top, covering in foil and allowing to rest for 10 minutes

The shrimp

  1. Thaw shrimp by placing into a strainer, and running under lukewarm water until soft
  2. Place a skillet onto the stove on medium heat with butter
  3. Once butter has melted, place shrimp into pan, and cook until shrimp is no longer pink
  4. Strain excess liquid in pan, and place the shrimp back into the pan
  5. Add desired spices
